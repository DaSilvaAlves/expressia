# Prompts Técnicos de Vídeo — iris Campaign v1.0

Estes prompts foram desenhados para serem utilizados em ferramentas de IA generativa de vídeo (como **Sora**, **Luma Dream Machine**, **Runway Gen-3** ou **Kling**) para criar os assets da campanha "Quiet Luxury".

## 1. Prompt de Background & Atmosfera (Base)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic high-end minimalist video, 4k resolution, 60fps. The background is an absolute deep pitch black (#000000). Fine particles of iridescent gold dust and subtle embers floating slowly across the screen in a zero-gravity environment. Ethereal, calm, and expensive atmosphere. Soft, out-of-focus bokeh of golden light in the far distance."

## 2. Prompt de Revelação do Logo (Gold Reveal)
> **Prompt:** "Macro shot of the center of a black screen. A sudden elegant explosion of fine gold glitter and stardust. As the dust clears, the word 'iris' emerges in a glowing, minimalist serif font (pure white). The lighting is soft and cinematic, highlighting the edges of the letters. The gold dust continues to swirl gently around the logo."

## 3. Prompt de Animação de Texto (Cursor)
> **Prompt:** "A clean black screen. A glowing white vertical line cursor (typewriter style) blinking rhythmically at 60bpm. As the cursor moves from left to right, elegant white serif typography (Lora font style) is typed out letter by letter. The animation is smooth and professional, reminiscent of a high-end terminal or a luxury brand announcement."

## 4. Prompt para Transições (Subtle Warp)
> **Prompt:** "A minimalist black screen with white text. A subtle, slow-motion 'warp' or 'dissolve' transition where the text turns into fine white smoke or light trails before the next line of text emerges from the shadows."

## 5. Prompt Cinematográfico: The Awakening (Trailer de Fundador)
Este prompt é desenhado para um vídeo de alto impacto, focado na jornada e transformação.

> **Prompt:** 
> **CHARACTER & BASIC SETUP**
> FACE REFER TO THE UPLOADED. Facial features and face shape must match exactly, without beautification. 
> **CLOTHING:** Black oversized hoodie, black oversized baggy jeans, black sneakers.
> **ATMOSPHERE & VISUAL STYLE:** Visual Tone: Cinematic widescreen transformation style. Simulate an IMAX film camera with a Panavision C-Series lens (35mm focal length, f/4).
> **Color Grading:** Low-saturation grey palette. Shadows are compressed but still keep visible detail.
> **Camera Rule:** Single continuous shot. No cuts.
> 
> **TIMELINE:**
> **0–3s: Impact & Awakening**
> The man crashes into broken rubble, rolling across cracked concrete. Dust bursts outward. He quickly rises to one knee, breathing heavily. In his hand, the small Obsidian Inferno Core pulses with molten red light. He whispers softly in a Portuguese (PT-PT) voice: «VAMOS A ISTO».
> 
> **3–10s: Transformation & Spear Manifestation**
> He raises his arm, making a sharp activation hand gesture with the relic. The Obsidian Inferno Core suddenly flares with violent crimson energy. Dark energy veins crawl across his arm as he throws his head back and screams. A pillar of red infernal light erupts upward. Molten sparks and black smoke swirl powerfully around his body. Fragments of obsidian armor begin forming, snapping onto his shoulders, torso, and arms. Jagged armor plates grow outward as glowing lava veins ignite between the seams, and his eyes ignite with deep crimson light. A single spear made of red kinetic energy forms in his hand.
> 
> **10s–end: Abomination Elimination**
> With newfound power and the kinetic spear, he lunges into a horde of demonic creatures. He executes devastating blows. The camera dynamically tracks him, weaving through the chaos as he effortlessly dispatches foes with precise strikes and bursts of infernal energy. Each elimination is swift and decisive, highlighting his overwhelming strength and agility. The creatures fly apart with every blow. The scene becomes a ballet of destruction, with the camera capturing every fluid motion and devastating impact.

## Dicas de Produção:
*   **Aspect Ratio:** Usar `--ar 16:9` para este trailer específico para obter o look IMAX/Cinemático.
*   **Música Sugerida:** Orquestral épica com elementos eletrónicos pesados (estilo Hans Zimmer / Junkie XL).
*   **Voz:** O sussurro «VAMOS A ISTO» deve soar profundo, determinado e puramente português de Portugal.
